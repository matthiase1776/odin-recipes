Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Enjoy pasta the way it was meant to be enjoyed

Ingredients

  • Update Ingredients
  • 500 g spaghetti
  • 125 ml Olive Oil
  • 30 g Parsley
  • 3 red peppers, fresh
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 pinch seasalt
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Prepare spaghetti al dente, as indicated on packaging
  2. Wash, dry, and finely chop parsley. Wash and seed the red peppers and dice finely
  3. Peel garlic and dice finely
  4. In small pan, over medium heat, heat olive oil
  5. Briefly add Garlic on it's own, then add peppers and parsley
  6. Stir until heated, add sea salt
  7. Add Oil mixture to prepared spaghetti and mix thoroughly
