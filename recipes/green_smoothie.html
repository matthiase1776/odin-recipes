Green Smoothie

This tart, satisfying, and delicious green smoothie blends spinach with pineapple, banana, and mint for a refreshing taste similar to a mojito!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups baby spinach leaves, or to taste
  • 1 (7 ounce) can crushed pineapple
  • Â½ cup water, or to taste
  • 1 banana, broken into chunks
  • 1 orange, peeled and segmented
  • 10 fresh mint leaves, or more to taste
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 lime, juiced

Steps

  1. Blend spinach, pineapple, water, banana, orange, mint, lemon juice, and lime juice in a blender until smooth.
  2. Add ice
  3. Blend again, until smooth
